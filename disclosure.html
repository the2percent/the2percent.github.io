Reporting boundaries
  • Fees are fully accounted for — ₹3,42,355 paid to brokers and exchanges over the study period. All figures on this site are net. Slippage and market impact are not modelled, as is standard practice in backtesting intraday strategies.
  • Returns are calculated on fixed capital (₹1L base). Position sizing does not compound with accumulated profits. This is a conservative reporting choice.
  • This is a trend-following system designed for directional markets. Mean-reverting or range-bound regimes present a different environment. Like all trend-following strategies, performance varies by market regime.
  • Leverage (5× intraday) is employed and sized deliberately. The historical capital floor of ₹1,03,656 reflects the minimum needed to survive every losing stretch in the record. Running below this level introduces risks not reflected in the published returns.
  • The proprietary signal logic is not disclosed on this site. Performance, methodology, and infrastructure are documented. The algorithm itself is not open source.
  • This page exists to document a methodology, demonstrate technical rigour, and present a transparent performance record — nothing more.
